ABOUT OUR CLINIC

Advanced Neonatal & Pediatric Care

With over 25 years of experience, Dr. Amit Upadhyay is a leading expert in Neonatology and Pediatrics, providing specialized care for newborns, premature babies, and children. Our center is equipped with one of the most advanced Level IIIB NICUs in Uttar Pradesh, ensuring the highest standards of safety and medical excellence.

  • Specialized care for premature & sick newborns
  • Advanced NICU with modern life-support systems
  • Comprehensive pediatric & vaccination services

MBBS, MD (Pediatrics), DNB, MNAMS, DM (Neonatology)

Dr. Amit Upadhyay

Dr. Amit Upadhyay is a highly experienced Neonatologist and Pediatrician with over 25 years of expertise in managing newborns, premature babies, and critically ill infants. He is the first DM Neonatologist in Meerut and Uttar Pradesh, trained at AIIMS, New Delhi and internationally in Australia.

  • Advanced Level IIIB NICU & critical care
  • Specialized care for premature & sick newborns
  • 40+ research publications & NNF Gold Medalist
  • 25+ years of trusted pediatric experience
