Neonatal Care

Neonatal Intensive Care (NICU)

Advanced Care Unit

Our Level IIIB Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) provides advanced medical care for premature and critically ill newborns. Equipped with high-frequency ventilators, modern monitoring systems, and specialized neonatal support, we ensure the best possible outcomes for newborn babies.

With over 25 years of experience, Dr. Amit Upadhyay leads a dedicated team focused on delivering safe, compassionate, and evidence-based neonatal care.

We specialize in managing high-risk newborns, including premature babies, low birth weight infants, and those requiring respiratory support. Our goal is to provide advanced treatment while ensuring comfort and care for both baby and parents.

  • Advanced Level IIIB NICU
  • High-frequency ventilation support
  • Care for premature & low birth weight babies
  • Infection control & asepsis protocols
  • 24/7 neonatal emergency care
  • Inhaled nitric oxide therapy
  • Nutrition & growth monitoring
  • Parent counseling & support

Our mission is to improve neonatal survival and provide world-class care for every newborn with compassion and excellence.

Neonatal Care

Premature Baby Care

Specialized Preemie Support

Babies born before 37 weeks require specialized care for healthy development. Our dedicated premature baby care program focuses on supporting organ development, preventing complications, and providing a nurturing environment for growth.

We offer kangaroo mother care, temperature stabilization, and gentle handling techniques to ensure optimal outcomes for your little one.

  • Kangaroo Mother Care (KMC)
  • Thermoregulation support
  • Developmental care techniques
  • Nutritional fortification
  • Apnea monitoring
  • Retinopathy of prematurity screening
  • Parent education & training
  • Follow-up growth tracking
Respiratory Care

Ventilation Support

Advanced Breathing Assistance

Our NICU is equipped with state-of-the-art conventional and high-frequency ventilators designed specifically for newborns. We provide life-saving respiratory support for babies with respiratory distress syndrome, pneumonia, or other breathing difficulties.

Our team is specially trained in gentle ventilation strategies that protect the developing lungs while ensuring adequate oxygen delivery.

  • Conventional mechanical ventilation
  • High-frequency oscillatory ventilation
  • Non-invasive ventilation (CPAP, BiPAP)
  • Inhaled nitric oxide therapy
  • Surfactant replacement therapy
  • 24/7 respiratory therapist support
  • Weaning protocols
  • Extubation readiness monitoring
Specialized Care

High-Risk Newborn Care

Complex Care Management

Babies with congenital conditions, birth asphyxia, sepsis, or surgical needs receive comprehensive multidisciplinary care from our expert team. We coordinate with pediatric specialists to provide seamless treatment.

Our approach focuses on aggressive early intervention, family-centered care, and long-term follow-up planning for babies with complex medical needs.

  • Neonatal neurology care
  • Management of neonatal seizures
  • Therapeutic hypothermia for HIE
  • Neonatal surgical consultation
  • Management of neonatal jaundice
  • Sepsis management
  • Parenteral nutrition
  • Neurodevelopmental follow-up

Preventive Care

Vaccination Programs

Complete Immunization Services

We follow the recommended vaccination schedule to protect children from vaccine-preventable diseases. Our vaccination programs are designed to be safe, convenient, and affordable for all families.

Well-Child Care

Growth Monitoring

Tracking Development Milestones

Regular growth monitoring is essential for early detection of nutritional or developmental issues. We track your child's height, weight, head circumference, and BMI percentiles using WHO standardized charts.

Our specialists provide guidance on nutrition, physical activity, and lifestyle to support optimal growth at every stage of childhood.

  • Periodic growth assessments
  • WHO growth chart analysis
  • Nutritional deficiency screening
  • Failure to thrive evaluation
  • Obesity prevention
  • Micronutrient supplementation
  • Dietary counseling for picky eaters
  • Pubertal growth tracking
